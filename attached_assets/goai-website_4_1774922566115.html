The Paradigm Shift
The enterprise delivery model hasn't evolved in 25 years. We built what comes next.

The traditional GDM — large offshore teams, linear handoffs, waterfall cadences — was designed for a world before AI. Today, a small AI-augmented pod can dramatically outperform teams many times its size — and we're seeing this in production at global enterprises today.

The Old World — GDM

Large teams. Linear handoffs. Diminishing returns.

  • 20–30 person teams for standard enterprise projects
  • Months of ramp-up before first deliverable
  • Communication overhead scales with headcount
  • Quality degrades as teams grow
  • Vendor lock-in through headcount dependency
The New World — APDM

Small pods. AI-native. Compounding velocity.

  • 4–6 person pods with AI force-multiplied output
  • First deliverable in days, not months
  • AI handles boilerplate; humans handle judgment
  • Quality improves as AI context deepens
  • Knowledge compounds across pod engagements

About goAI

We didn't set out to build a services company. We set out to end the need for one.

goAI Solutions is an AI-native consulting and delivery studio. We were founded on a contrarian belief: that the Global Delivery Model — the backbone of the $200B+ IT services industry — is fundamentally broken in the age of AI.

The GDM was built for a world where human typing speed was the bottleneck. More people meant more output. But AI has changed the equation. Today, the bottleneck is judgment, not keystrokes. A small team with the right AI tooling and deep organizational context can outperform a team ten times its size.

We built the AI Pod Delivery Model (APDM) to prove it. And then we proved it — for Fortune 50 retailers, for one of the world's largest energy utilities, for a global media conglomerate. Not in pilots. In production. At scale.

APDM doesn't just give developers better tools. It rewires how enterprise software gets built — connecting AI to your codebase, your architecture, your compliance requirements, and your business rules, so a 4-person pod can operate with the context and output of a team five times its size.

We operate across four continents — India, the United States, Brazil, and Australia — serving enterprises in energy, retail, financial services, media, and beyond.
